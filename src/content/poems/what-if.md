---
title: what if?
date: 2024-12-17
draft: false
---
sometimes i wonder, what if<br />
i was born in a simpler time.<br />
in a faraway place, away from the city<br />
where the streams are still blue, the air still clean.<br />
the breeze is cool and the fields are green<br />
chirping of birds replace the cacophony of traffic.<br />
there is no hurry to get somewhere<br />
nor fear someone will get there first<br />
life is at a still, but still i am happy.<br />
<br />

the night falls and silence prevails<br />
the stars light up the night sky<br />
in lieu of the grayness of haze.<br />
maybe i lay back and look up<br />
at the million little twinkles and wonder<br />
what if i was born faraway, in the city.<br />
