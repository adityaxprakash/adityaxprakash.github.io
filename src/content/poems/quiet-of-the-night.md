i find solace in the quiet of the night.<br />
the silence is a treat to the ears,<br />
the darkness a feast for the eyes.<br />
<br />

my mind likes to wander far and wide,<br />
think about important things,<br />
think about not so important things,<br />
think about what-ifs and had-beens,<br />
think about all that i want to do,<br />
think about all that i have to do,<br />
<br />

my mind is abuzz now, i cannot sleep<br />
i am falling into an endless abyss,<br />
it is dark and quiet, i must get out —<br />
i should talk to someone, i think<br />
but i like the solitude, i think<br />
i face a strange dichotomy now<br />
of wanting to be left alone, but not to be alone.<br />
<br />

i think too much, i think<br />
the quiet of the night is driving me crazy.<br />
