and i want to be many things —<br />
i want to read every line and know every word.<br />
i want to be everywhere and be everyone.<br />
i want to eat every food and smell every smell.<br />
i want to feel every emotion and live every moment.<br />
but i am stuck here as myself and can’t seem to change.
